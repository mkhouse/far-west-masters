Happy New Year! Our racing season kicks off next week at Sugar Bowl. Whether you're a seasoned racer or joining us for the first time, we're excited to see you on the hill.

Sugar Bowl Slalom Races: Our first SL races of the season are at Sugar Bowl on Saturday-Sunday, January 10-11. Early registration deadline is 7pm Sunday, January 4. Final registration deadline is 7pm Thursday, January 8. Entry fee is $45 per race. See the event flyer for full details.

Register for Sugar Bowl SL Jan 10-11

Mammoth Super G Races: Two days of speed racing at Mammoth Mountain on Friday-Saturday, January 23-24, with two SG races each day. The first SG race each day counts toward the 2025 Masters National Speed Series. Early registration deadline is 7pm Sunday, January 18. Final registration deadline is 7pm Tuesday, January 20. Entry fee is $55 per race. See the event flyer for full details.

Register for Mammoth SG Jan 23-24

Write a Race Report!

We need race reporters! If you're racing at Sugar Bowl or Mammoth and would like to write about your experience, please email Melissa at forerunner@farwestmasters.org. Reports are short (250-500 words) and first-time writers are welcome. We're happy to help with editing.

Photos are also welcome, with or without a report. Action shots, podium moments, or anything that captures race day, send them our way.
