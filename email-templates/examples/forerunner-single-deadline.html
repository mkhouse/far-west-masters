Mammoth Super G Races Jan 23-24

We have two days of speed racing at Mammoth Mountain on Friday-Saturday, January 23-24, with two SG races each day. Early registration deadline is 7pm TODAY Sunday, January 18. Final registration deadline is 7pm Tuesday, January 20. Entry fee is $55 per race, and goes up to $60 on Mon 1/19. Registration is open now! Check out the event info flyer for details.

If you are racing at Mammoth, be sure to review these important SG/DH Race Rule Reminders.

Register for Mammoth SG Jan 23-24

SG Training & Venue Info

Training Session Thursday 1/22 12 - 2:30pm: Mammoth is offering a SG training session on Thursday, providing all racers competing in this weekend's SG races an opportunity for speed training on the race venue. Cost is $30; check in and pay at the Mammoth Race Department office on Thursday morning. The Race Department is to the right of the Broadway chair as you're looking up the mountain.

Race Venue: The SG race venue is World Cup into Terrie's. Start is at "air jump" at the base of Cornice Bowl. Access via Broadway Express (Ch. 1) from Main Lodge and Face Lift Express (Ch. 3); traverse on the backside of Ch. 3 to the start. Alternate access: gondola or Ch. 23 to the top, ski Cornice Bowl down to the start.

Customize Your Jackets with FWM Logo!

For those of us that have too many jackets but still want to represent FWM, we were able to get the embroidery files from Sync so you can customize your gear at a local shop. The file includes both the small, chest logo and the rear FWM Ski Racing text. The shop will need the .dst file but go ahead and download and provide them the entire folder to ensure they have everything they need.

You can find the file on the FWM Merch website by scrolling to the bottom.

If you have it done, please let us know how it turned out! Share it with us on Instagram @far_west_masters, send Melissa photos and let us know what shop you went to, how much it cost, how long it took, etc.
