November 27, 2025 Newsletter

Happy Thanksgiving! There's a lot happening this week as we head into the start of the racing season.

Mammoth Race Camp: Registration for the December 2-5 Mammoth Masters Race Camp closes Saturday, November 29. If you're planning to attend, sign up now!

Mammoth December 6-7 Races: Early registration deadline is 7pm Sunday, November 30. Final registration deadline is 7pm Thursday, December 4. Register early to help our race organizers plan!

The FIS Alpine World Cup is racing at Copper Mountain, Colorado this weekend! Watch live for free on Outside+ (just enter an email address) or on demand with an Outside+ subscription. NBC and CNBC also have broadcast coverage.
