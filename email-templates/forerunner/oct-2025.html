October, 2025 Forerunner

Welcome to the new Far West Masters website and Forerunner Newsletter!

The team of Melissa, Deb, Damian, and Hamish has completely redesigned and relaunched the Far West Masters website. We hope this updated site will be easier to navigate and find information about what's going on. If you have questions about the new website, email webmaster@farwestmasters.org.

The Forerunenr newsletter is also getting a makeover, with articles highlighted in email and links to stories posted on the website. This gives the Forerunner editor (Melissa) a simpler way to get timely content posted for everyone and a better way to announce with email. If you have questions about our newsletter, or if you'd like to write a story for the Forerunner, email forerunner@farwestmasters.org.

Don't forget it's time to renew your membershp! You need both a U.S. Ski and Snowboard membership and a Far West Masters membership to participate in our races. Click below for instructions.

How to renew your membership

We created a 2025 Far West Masters Season Survey where you can provide feedback on your experience this season and also give us ideas for what we should focus on for next year. If you could take 5 minutes to complete the survey it is greatly appreciated, and if you have skills or time to help out please let us know and we will follow up with you individually. Please take it only once.
